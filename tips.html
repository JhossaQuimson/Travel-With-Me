Top 5 Travel Tips

  • Pack Light: Only bring essentials to make moving around easier.
  • Stay Safe: Keep copies of important documents and be mindful in crowded areas.
  • Budget Wisely: Track your expenses and save for emergencies.
  • Learn Local Phrases: Basic greetings and phrases go a long way in building rapport.
  • Travel Off-Peak: Visit attractions early or in the off-season for fewer crowds.
